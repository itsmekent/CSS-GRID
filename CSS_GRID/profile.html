Team Profiles

Mike Johnson

Akira Nakai is a Japanese car tuner and founder of **RAUH-Welt Begriff (RWB)**, known for his unique wide-body Porsche 911 customizations. His distinctive style combines aggressive design and precision, attracting a global fan base. Nakai handcrafts each RWB Porsche himself, creating one-of-a-kind vehicles that merge Japanese car culture with classic German engineering.

George Barris

George Barris, known as the "King of the Kustomizers," was a legendary custom car builder famous for creating iconic Hollywood vehicles like the Batmobile from the 1960s *Batman* series, the Munster Koach, and the original KITT from *Knight Rider*. His bold, imaginative designs set the standard for custom cars.

Chip Foose

Chip Foose is a highly respected custom car designer known for his elegant, clean style. Learning from his father, Sam Foose, he gained fame on the TV show *Overhaulin'*, where he transformed ordinary cars into stunning customs. His work, blending modern and classic elements, has earned him numerous awards and a dedicated fan base.

Jesse James

Jesse James, founder of West Coast Choppers, gained fame for his custom motorcycles and expanded into custom cars. Known for his rebellious, industrial design style, his TV shows *Monster Garage* and *Jesse James is a Dead Man* showcased his craftsmanship, blending traditional hot rod influences with a modern, gritty aesthetic.
